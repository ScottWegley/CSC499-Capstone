Permutation Cracker

This page hosts a tool for cracking text encrypted by a Permutation Cipher. Due to the nature of a Permutation Cipher, it will crack text encrypted by a Caesar Cipher too. Tooltips on every element are enabled by default and recommended if this is your first time with this tool. Hover over an element to see these tips. For this tool to work, I recommend that you use an incredibly lengthy word list for permutation cracking. If a word in your encrypted text is not in the dictionary you use, this tool may not work. Your browser may stall for up to 10 minutes while running a number of the functions of this page; give it time to finish.
